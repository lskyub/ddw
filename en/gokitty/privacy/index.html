Privacy Policy

How Go Kitty protects your personal information

Last Updated: January 2, 2025 | Effective Date: January 2, 2025
üõ°Ô∏è Your Privacy Matters
We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This policy explains our practices in simple, clear terms.

1. Information We Collect

Game Data

  • High scores and game statistics
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Audio settings (music/sound effects preferences)

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App performance and crash reports
  • Network connectivity status

Optional Data

  • Advertisement interaction data (only when you choose to watch ads)
  • Analytics data to improve game performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Save your game progress and high scores
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Show relevant advertisements (with your consent)
  • Analyze game usage to enhance features

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the game (like ad networks, analytics services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Backup: Optional cloud sync for game progress (when available)
  • Security Measures: Industry-standard encryption and security practices
  • Data Retention: We keep data only as long as necessary for game functionality

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Decline optional data collection
  • Ad Control: Choose whether to watch advertisements

6. Children's Privacy

Go Kitty is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our game may include third-party services:

  • Ad Networks: For displaying advertisements
  • Analytics: For understanding game usage
  • Crash Reporting: For improving game stability

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you of significant changes through the app
  • Maintain a history of policy versions

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ddwservices.com
  • Support: support@ddwservices.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
